My Journey

A story of growth, challenges, and unforgettable moments.

1. The Early Years: Discovering the World

I was born and raised in Compostela, Cebu. As a kid, I was always curious, running around, asking endless questions, and finding joy in the simplest things. My elementary days were filled with laughter, games, and the first lessons of responsibility.

2. High School: A Time of Change

High school was a mix of fun and challenges. I learned the importance of friendship, experienced my first academic struggles, and started discovering my true interests. It was during these years that I realized my passion for learning and personal growth.

3. Senior High: Stepping Closer to the Future

Senior high school was where things got serious. I had to make important decisions about my future. There were stressful nights, endless requirements, and moments of doubt, but in the end, I pushed through, knowing I was getting closer to my dreams.

4. College: The Real Challenge Begins

Now, in my third year of college, the challenges have doubled, but so has my determination. Balancing academics, personal growth, and friendships is tough, but every struggle is shaping me into the person I want to become.

5. Lessons and Dreams

Through all the ups and downs, one thing remains true: Life is a journey, and I’m excited for what’s next. Every failure is a lesson, and every success is a step closer to my dreams. The journey continues, and I can’t wait to see where it leads me.
